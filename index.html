About Rachel and Tony

The Start
Tony & Rachel first met in February 2011 at UC Berkeley's Department of Chemical Engineering grad school visit weekend, where Rachel was an overly enthusiastic prospective student and Tony an experienced third year. There they spent an hour having a great conversation at Prof. Smit's house, though Rachel has no memory of this. When Rachel came to Berkeley for grad school, they re-met at a College of Chemistry Electric Fielders summer softball game. After a second great conversation, Tony got Rachel's phone number and texted her to invite her to a barbeque that weekend. Unfortunately, Rachel accidentally gave him the wrong number and both of them wondered why they never heard from the other! Luckily, they ran into each other in the hallway at the College of Chemistry and figured out their mistake.
Though they saw each other again at numerous ChemE social events, including singing Journey together at the Shattuck Down-Low for Barbara's birthday and dancing together at the Graduate Assembly Social at Kip's, they like to remember their first date as being at Bette's Oceanview Diner, where Tony took Rachel to breakfast at what continues to be their favorite brunch place. After they finished eating, Rachel asked what they should do next, to which he replied "Well, I kinda want to go to the lumber yard." So, after a delicious brunch at an upscale restaurant on 4th street, they spent the second part of their date wandering around MacBeath's Hardwood comparing the prices of walnut versus cherry for Tony's latest woodworking project. When Rachel was game to spend a Saturday afternoon picking out rough-cut lumber, he knew he had found a special girl!
The Follow-up
After a second, more typical, date at Esperpento in San Francisco, and a third hiking in Tilden Regional Park, they quickly started spending most of their time together. Tony became a frequent visitor to Ward Street and Rachel was always popping by the Newman lab and hanging out at 1257 Francisco where Tony lived. During these times, they found out that they got along really well with each other's friends, something that was very important to both of them. Alan, Craig, Cynthia, Hedvig, Lin, Nate and Rachel C. all spent a lot of time with Tony and Rachel during those crucial first few months of dating, and together they helped set a solid foundation of laughter and trust for Tony and Rachel's developing relationship.
In the subsequent years, Tony and Rachel have had many adventures together. They have taken trips both near and far to Chicago, Malta and the Amalfi coast, Thailand, Barbados, Wenatchee, Montana, Bali and many more. They have cooked hundred of meals and desserts together, built furniture together, gone on road trips together, discovered new restaurants and bars together, hiked new trails together, been silly together, run a half marathon together, played board games together, and overall spent an incredible 4 years.
The Proposal
After spending Christmas in Switzerland visiting their friends, they traveled to Paris where Tony proposed to Rachel on January 1st, 2015 in the garden at the point of the Île de la Cité. It was a beautiful winter day and they were sharing a typical French picnic of brie, smoked salmon, baguette and Rachel's favorite Jurançon wine. Tony gave Rachel a beautiful wooden box he had made out of cherry and purple heart and had inlaid with her initials. When she opened it, she saw the incredible diamond ring he had designed for her with rose gold, white gold, and small purple sapphires. Rachel immediately said "YES!" before Tony even had a chance to ask the question that he had been preparing for the last 3 months.
